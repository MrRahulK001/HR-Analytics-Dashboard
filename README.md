. HR-Analytics-Dashboard | Power BI

This project presents a professional HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI.
The dashboard is designed to help organizations analyze employee data and derive
actionable insights related to attrition, income distribution, job satisfaction,
and workforce demographics.


 . Project Objectives
- Monitor overall employee strength and attrition rate
- Analyze average monthly income across departments
- Understand job satisfaction levels of employees
- Identify attrition trends by department and age group
- Enable data-driven HR decision-making through interactive visuals

. Key Metrics & KPIs
- Total Employees
- Average Monthly Income
- Attrition Percentage
- Average Job Satisfaction Score

.  Dashboard Analysis
- Department-wise attrition analysis
- Monthly employee trend analysis
- Income distribution by department
- Job satisfaction level segmentation
- Attrition by age group and gender

. Interactivity & Filters
- Department filter
- Gender filter
- Dynamic visuals for deep-dive analysis

.  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Measures & Calculated Columns)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization & KPI Design
